## Three-minute demo script

### 0:00–0:25 — The problem

"When I sit down to work, I have scattered tasks and limited energy. Existing tools make me decide what to do, manage a timer, and track progress separately. Zenflow turns that into one focused loop."

### 0:25–1:05 — AI makes the decision

Open the AI Focus Coach. Add two or three realistic tasks, choose a low or medium energy level, and generate a roadmap. Point out that the plan changes its focus-block and break cadence according to the energy level and open tasks.

### 1:05–1:40 — The plan becomes action

Adopt the roadmap into the task board. Pin the top task, then open the Wave Focus Timer and start a session. Briefly show the optional soundscape as a focus aid, not as the product's main feature.

### 1:40–2:15 — The outcome is tracked

Complete a task or show a completed focus session. Open Focus Statistics to show focus minutes, sessions, tasks completed, and streak information.

### 2:15–2:45 — The retention loop

Reload the app and show that the task board, focus state, and settings persist. If the hosted AI key is configured, generate one more plan without entering a visitor API key.

### 2:45–3:00 — Closing

"Zenflow is not another timer. It uses AI to decide what deserves your next hour, helps you follow through, and shows that the hour produced progress."
